Types of Woodwinds & DIY Guide 🌿

Overtone Flute 🎶

Base Material: Hollowed bamboo/PVC pipe + Wooden block
  • 1. Prepare the pipe
  • 2. Cut the fipple
  • 3. (Optional) Add extra drilling holes! 🛠️

Reed-Based Woodwind 🍃

Base Material: Dried cane reeds
  • 1. Tie reed to mouthpiece
  • 2. Use reed vibration for sound production

Bamboo Flute 🌿

Base Material: Tape (similar to reed but different vibration method)
  • 1. Measure hole spacing
  • 2. Mark and drill holes
  • 3. Add tape for tuning! 🎀
